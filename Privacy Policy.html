Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

SiteWOF ("we", "our", or "us") is a website audit tool that helps users analyze website performance, compliance, and technical issues. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • Email addresses — when you request a free audit code
  • Website URLs — the URLs you submit for audit
  • Payment information — processed securely by Stripe; we do not store credit card details
  • Usage data — limited to tracking free audit usage per code to prevent abuse

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver audit codes to your email
  • To process payments via Stripe
  • To enforce one-free-audit-per-user limits
  • To generate and deliver website audit reports

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

SiteWOF relies on the following third-party services:

  • Stripe — payment processing (Stripe Privacy Policy)
  • Google PageSpeed Insights API — performance auditing (Google Privacy Policy)
  • Cloudflare — infrastructure and free tier tracking (Cloudflare Privacy Policy)
  • DeepSeek API — AI-powered report generation

Each of these services operates under their own privacy policies.

4. Cookies

SiteWOF itself does not set cookies. Our payment processor (Stripe) may use cookies necessary for secure checkout. Our hosting platform (GitHub Pages) may set minimal technical cookies.

5. Data Retention

Email addresses used for free audit codes are retained only to prevent duplicate claims. Payment records are retained by Stripe per their policies. Audit results are generated in real-time and not stored by us.

6. Your Rights

Under the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020, you have the right to:

  • Request access to personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of your personal information
  • Make a complaint if you believe your privacy has been breached

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Security

All traffic to SiteWOF is encrypted via HTTPS. Payments are processed securely through Stripe's PCI-compliant infrastructure. We take reasonable steps to protect your information but no online service is 100% secure.
